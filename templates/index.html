Multi-Character View

Switch between linked pilots quickly, or use the overview to scan who needs attention first.

Planetary Industry Timing

Wallet Awareness

What You Need

1. Create an EVE application

Use the EVE developer portal and set the callback URL to http://127.0.0.1:5000/callback.

2. Add your local credentials

Fill in your local .env with your client ID, client secret, redirect URI, and Flask secret key.

3. Approve the required scopes
